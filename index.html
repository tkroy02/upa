About Me

Learn about my qualifications, experience, and teaching philosophy

Hello! I'm Taiwo Ogunlaiye, the founder of Tklesson. I hold a Master's degree in Economics (2022) from the University of Windsor, Canada, and two Bachelor's degrees in Mechanical Engineering (2013) and Economics (2021).

A testament to my work ethic and value for education, I graduated as the overall Valedictorian and earned First Class honors for my BSc in Economics in 2021.

With over 12 years of classroom teaching and 16 years of online tutoring, I specialize in helping students master challenging topics in Physics, Math, Chemistry, Biology, Economics, and Advanced Placement (AP) courses.
